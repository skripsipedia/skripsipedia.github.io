Jawab :
log 9 / log 27
= log 3² / log 3³
= (2. log 3) / (3 . log 3) <-- ingat sifat log a^n = n. log a
= 2/3

= -1/4 (14 - 6√5)
= -7/2 + 3/2√5
= (3√5 - 7)/2
