PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin, aktivitas sehari-hari dan fakta.

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es)

(-) S + do/does + not + V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Catatan

➡ Jika Subjek singular “He, She, it, satu nama” menggunakan Verb+s

1. Ditto plays football

2. He sends some money

3. Marina swims twice a week

5. Aunt Liza waters the floor every morning

6. The man sits alone

8. Mrs. Sulis always buys fried banana in the morning

10. Bella usually browses some information in the internet

➡ Jika Subjek Plural “I, You, they, 2 nama” menggunakan Verb-1

4. Dina and Karla make juice every afternoon

7. My parent(s) attend a wedding party

9. I type some drama scripts
