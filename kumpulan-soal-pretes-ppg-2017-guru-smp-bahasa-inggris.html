How to write dates in British English : date (in ordinal number) + month + year

1. 23rd January 2017 (spoken : the twenty third of March, twenty seventeen or two thousand seventeen)

2. 27th March 1963 (the twenty seventh of March, nineteen sixty three)

3. 1st October 2013 (the first of October, two thousand thirteen)

4. 26th February 1980 (the twenty sixth of February, nineteen eighty)

5. 21st April 1966 (the twenty first of April, nineteen sixty six)


How to write dates in American English : month + date (in ordinal number), year

1. January 23rd, 2017 (spoken : March twenty third, twenty seventeen or two thousand seventeen)
