2. 5 soal pilihan ganda tentang compliment


Multiple Choice Questions about ComplimentJawaban
Pendahuluan

Compliment adalah sebuah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan pujian kepada seseorang atas apa yang dikerjakan, penampilan seseorang, dll. Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat pujian yang paling sering digunakan:

1. How + Adjective + Noun + is/are

Contoh:

How beautiful your dress isHow fancy your cars areHow tasty your pizza is

2. What + Adjective + Noun.

Contoh:

What a big house!What a wonderful world!What a beautiful day!

3. Noun Phrase + is/look + (really) + Adjective.

Contoh:

Your suit is really shinyHis acting is awesome

4. Noun/Pronoun + (really) + like/love + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

I like your cooking a lot.We love your cocktail party.

5. Pronoun + is + (really) + Adjective + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

That is a really cute sunglassesThis is a pretty flower
Pembahasan

33. 10 Soal Bahasa Inggris tentang compliment


compliment Is 1.an expression of praise, commendation, or admiration:A sincere compliment boosts one's morale.2.a formal act or expression of civility, respect, or regard:The mayor paid him the compliment of escorting him.3.compliments, a courteous greeting; good wishes; regards:He sends you his compliments.4.Archaic. a gift; present.verb (used with object)5.to pay a compliment to:She complimented the child on his good behavior.6.to show kindness or regard for by a gift or other favor:He complimented us by giving a party in our honor.7.to congratulate; felicitate:to compliment a prince on the birth of a son.verb (used without object)8.to pay compliments.
